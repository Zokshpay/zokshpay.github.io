FAQs

Get all your queries about Zoksh answered here

Is Zoksh user-friendly for newbies and non-crypto users?

We take pride in the simplicity of our platform. We have tried to design every interface and integration in line with the established products and practices in traditional payment systems. Any Web-2 business can start accepting crypto payments in as little as 15 mins.

What makes Zoksh an excellent choice for businesses and consumers? Does It have some helpful features and functions that businesses find really attractive and useful?

Zoksh allows businesses to accept crypto payments without any hassle and currently supports 16 blockchains so businesses can effortlessly receive payments from different platforms. Plus they don't have to worry about continue adding/improve network support as new blockchains come along in the future. They can serve the fragmented user base across different blockchains with one single platform. An end consumer can easily pay in any of the tokens allowed by the merchant.

What inspired you to create Zoksh and what sets it apart from other payment platforms?

We started Zoksh to allow charitable organizations to easily accept donations in crypto. But soon realized that there was a much bigger opportunity to work on. We are completely non-custodial and we are not tying an end-user or a business to one particular blockchain network.

What are your plans to drive the adoption and use of the Zoksh pay platform?

Zoksh being a B2B business we are focused on building channel partnerships.

What is Zoksh doing to address the challenges and opportunities presented by the rapidly evolving mobile payments landscape?

We are currently working on our mobile SDKs to simplify payments on mobile platforms. We are also incorporating more and more mobile payment technologies like we also recently integrated SolanaPay into our system.

Can you explain the level of customizability and support that Zoksh Pay's ramp solution provides for integrating with different payment methods across the globe?

Our on-ramp aggregator solution can be easily plugged into any wallet or website. we can control payment methods/networks/tokens/geographies it's highly customizable.

How does Zoksh payment gateway system enable in-store and online merchants to receive crypto payments from customers?

We provide different APIs and plugins that can be easily integrated by merchants to receive payments from customers across the globe.

How does Zoksh safeguard against data breaches and unauthorized access to sensitive information?

We do not store any personally identifiable information on our systems.

Do you have certain mechanisms that can ensure the safety of transactions and funds? What kind of partnerships do you have in terms of security?

Our system is a simplified layer on top of actual blockchain transactions. We are partnered with leading crypto firms like coinfirm & chainanalysis to help prevent bad actors.

Is it possible to get a refund if a payment problem occurs?

Yes definitely the merchant can issue a refund if any payment problem occurs.

How responsive is your team to resolving issues or addressing user concerns? What type of customer support does Zoksh offer?

We currently support online chat which is available from the payment page itself and You can normally expect a response within a few hours.

How does Zoksh differentiate itself from other existing payment solutions in the market, especially those that already provide crypto payment processing or automated market making?

Zoksh differentiates itself by being the first Non-Custodial payment solution in the market. The payment rails and settlement systems are implemented via smart contracts so only merchants have control over their funds.

Using blockchain technology how does Zoksh aim to bring financial inclusion to the unbanked population?

Zoksh enables crypto payments for merchants across the world and we are giving access to products and services being offered by our merchants to the unbanked population across the globe.

How does Zoksh manage international transactions and currency conversions? Are there any fees associated with these transactions?

We have partnered with some of the leading On-Ramp & Off-Ramp providers. Which helps our customers to get access to the best rates in the industry.

Zoksh supports multiple Fiat and Cryptocurrencies and how easy it is to make payments in local currency, and do we have to go through verification or not?

While paying with fiat payment methods like credit/debit cards, bank transfers, pix or similar, there is a one-time KYC required at present.

Currently how many blockchains does the Zoksh platform support?

We currently support 16 blockchains i.e Bitcoin, Ethereum, Litecoin, Solana, Polygon, Bitcoin Cash, Binance Chain, Near, Avalanche, Dogecoin, Aurora, OKX Chain, Hedera Hashgraph, Moonbeam, RSK Chain & Celo. We are always expanding our currency support based on transaction volume and demand.
